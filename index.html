SOME OF THE MOST FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS ABOUT OUR COMPANY ARE ANSWERED HERE.

Can you produce items not shown on your website?

Yes, we can produce any types of leather goods as per our honorable consumer requirement which are not presented at our web site.

What are your lead times for bulk manufacture?

After confirming of sample we need 5 to 7 weeks to complete your bulk production.

What materials do you use to produce items and are they ethically sourced?

We use 100 % genuine leather & source all other relevant material from our reliable sources.

What is your minimum order quantity?

You can order any of your desired goods minimum 100 PCS per style per color.

Can you produce samples and prototypes?

Yes, we offer samples & prototypes before going to bulk production for you.

How long do samples take to produce?

After confirming your desired design, we need Maximum 2/3 weeks to produce and deliver your sample.

Are there any import duties I should know about?

It depends on your country’s government import rules & regulations.

Is my Intellectual Property protected?

Yes, your all intellectual property & confidential matter always protected by us.

How do you accept payment?

The Normal payment terms is 30 % deposit before production and 70 % balance before shipment. We Accept T/T, L/C, Western Union, PayPal right now.
